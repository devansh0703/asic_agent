Design a 4-bit synchronous up counter with clock, reset, and enable inputs.

The counter should:
- Have inputs: clk (clock), rst (reset), enable
- Have output: count (4-bit)
- Reset to 0 when rst is high
- Increment on each clock edge when enable is high
- Wrap around from 15 to 0
- Hold value when enable is low

Use synchronous reset and non-blocking assignments.
